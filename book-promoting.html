Promoting Multilingualism in Schools: A Framework for Implementing the Seal of Biliteracy

Authors
Kristin J. Davin & Amy J. Heineke
Publisher
ACTFL Press
Published
2022
Ideal For
K-12 Administrators World Language Teachers Bilingual Program Coordinators

A comprehensive framework for implementing the Seal of Biliteracy in K-12 settings. This resource guides educators through understanding biliteracy policy, recognizing students' multilingual strengths, and building sustainable programs. It includes practical tools, assessment strategies, and real-world examples from schools successfully implementing the Seal.

The Research Behind This Book

This framework synthesizes research from over a decade of study on K-12 Seal of Biliteracy implementation, including work on program evaluation, student assessment, and policy barriers. The authors have conducted extensive fieldwork with educators and administrators to ensure the framework is grounded in real-world practice and evidence-based approaches.
