Publisher

Ideal For

This edited volume examines the Seal of Biliteracy through case studies of policy implementation in diverse contexts across the United States. It presents real-world examples from districts and states navigating this policy initiative, highlighting successes, challenges, and lessons learned. The book provides valuable insights for policymakers and administrators considering or refining their own Seal implementation strategies.

The Research Behind This Book
