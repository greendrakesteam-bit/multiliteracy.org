Pathways to the Seal of Biliteracy

Georgetown University Press

A roadmap for advancing equitable access to biliteracy. This book provides foundational understanding, practical examples, and key resources to help parents, educators, and policymakers build pathways to the Seal of Biliteracy in elementary and middle schools, covering bilingual, heritage, and world language education settings.

The Seal of Biliteracy in Higher Education

Routledge

This volume brings together the work of those implementing, using, or researching the Seal of Biliteracy in higher education, exploring how colleges and universities can leverage the Global Seal to promote advocacy within and across communities. Readers learn about potential pathways, partnerships, and possibilities for biliteracy at the postsecondary level.

Information Age Publishing / Emerald

This edited volume examines the Seal of Biliteracy through case studies of policy implementation in diverse contexts across the United States. It expands the literature by presenting real-world examples from districts and states navigating this relatively new policy initiative.
