The Research Behind This Book

This framework is informed by over a decade of peer-reviewed research, including studies on student voice, policy assessment variation, and K-12 implementation challenges. The authors draw on extensive fieldwork and collaborative partnerships with educators and administrators implementing the Seal of Biliteracy in real-world settings.
