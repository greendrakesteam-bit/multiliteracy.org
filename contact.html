Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for speaking engagement inquiries?

Dr. Davin typically requires 6-8 weeks advance notice for speaking engagements and professional development workshops to ensure availability and adequate preparation.

Do you provide custom professional development sessions?

Yes, Dr. Davin works with schools and organizations to develop customized professional development sessions tailored to your institution's specific needs and goals related to biliteracy implementation.

Are virtual presentations available?

Yes, Dr. Davin is available for both in-person and virtual presentations, workshops, and consultations. Virtual options provide flexibility for organizations across the country.

How can our organization request Dr. Davin for a specific event?

Please send an email to kdavin@charlotte.edu with details about your event, including the date, location (or virtual platform), audience, and topics of interest. Dr. Davin will respond within one business week.

Response Time

Dr. Davin strives to respond to all inquiries within one business week. For time-sensitive requests, please mark your email as urgent.
